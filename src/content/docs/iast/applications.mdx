The applications page informs you about the total number of found vulnerabilites in your tested applications and the number of untested applications you have. You can always add untested applications to the New Relic IAST capability.

## Details and remediation of an application [#remediation-app]

Check out all the issues detected with our New Relic IAS testing on your applications.

To see the details and the remediation instructions for an application:

1. Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com) > All capabilities > IAST** and click **Applications** on the left navigation pane.

2. Select an application. You can order all the columns alphabetically.

## Enable New Relic IAST on an untested application [#enable-iast]

To enable IAST on an untested application from the summary page:

1. Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com) > All capabilities > IAST** and click **Applications** on the left navigation pane.

2. Click on the **Untested applications** tab and select the application you want to enable New Relic IAST.

3. In the **Enable IAST** window, follow the steps to update your application configuration so it can be tested for exploitable vulnerabilities.

    <img
        title="Enable IAST for your untested application"
        alt="Enable IAST for your untested application"

    />

4. Update the APM agent if it's needed.

5. Click **Next**.

6. Follow the instructions to update the configuration.

7. Click **Next**.

8. Restart your application and click **See your data**.
